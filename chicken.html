CHICKEN CATHEDRAL

Built project, 2019

Almonte, Ontario

The design process started with a physical model.

The curved framing is made of plywood, cut into shape using a jigsaw and insulated using fiberglass. The single-curved shape made it possible to attach strapping and shingles like a planar roof. For the cedar siding, I used a Japanese charring method, although the colour washed off within a month and had to be painted.
